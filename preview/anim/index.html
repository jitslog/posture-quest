Glute bridge
Arms flat at sides. Drive heels, squeeze glutes, lift hips to a line.
Hip-flexor stretch
Knee over ankle. Tuck tailbone, glide hips forward. Feel the front of the back hip.
Dead bug
Back flat on floor. Lower opposite arm + leg, then return. Keep the low back pinned.
Plank
Forearms down. Tuck tailbone, squeeze glutes + abs. Flat line, head to heels.
Lower-back release (child's pose)
Sit hips back toward heels, arms long, let the lower back round. Breathe.
Posterior pelvic tilt
On your back, knees bent. Flatten the low back into the floor, hold, release.
Standing wall posture hold
Heels, butt, upper back, head on the wall. Tuck tailbone to shrink the low-back gap.
Bird-dog
All fours, flat back. Reach opposite arm + leg out level, hold 2s. Don't let hips rock.
Back extension
Face down, hands by temples. Lift your chest with your back, pause, lower slowly. Neck long.
Side plank
On one forearm, body in a straight line, hips lifted. Don't let the hips sag.
Chin tuck
Eyes level. Glide your head straight back (double chin), hold 2s, release.
Doorway pec stretch
Forearms on a doorframe, step through and lean. Feel it across the chest. Don't shrug.
Scap squeeze
Pull elbows back and down, squeezing your shoulder blades together. Don't shrug up.
