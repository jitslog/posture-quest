Glute bridge
Arms flat at your sides on the floor. Drive through heels, squeeze glutes, lift hips to a straight line.
12 reps · hands stay flat on the floor
Hip-flexor stretch (kneeling)
Front knee stays over your ankle (don't push it past your toes). Tuck your tailbone and glide your hips forward — feel the stretch in the FRONT of the back hip.
30 seconds each side · knee stays put, hips glide forward
